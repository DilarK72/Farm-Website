About us

What is Dernega?

Dernega is a Peruvian business association founded in 2002 which consists of the transportation of dairy goods and livestock management.

Dernega stands for:

"Diego y Edson Ramos: Asociacion Ganadera," translated into English as "Diego & Edson Ramos Livestock Association." Edson is my father's name, who founded this business and carries my name and his until nowadays.

Location of the business:

This business is headquartered in the Lurin district of Lima, the capital of Peru. This rural area is distinguishable because of other farms, businesses, and the country's life.
